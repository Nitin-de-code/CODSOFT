Screenshots

Description

Ever wanted to try out your basic knowledge on a specific subject,well this quiz application does just that,it tests your basic knowledge on a specific subject based on the final score you get.This quiz consists of 7 categories and each one have their own set of 50 different questions,of which 10 questions would be asked, each question would have 4 options out of which one is correct and the rest are wrong, for every right question +10 points are given out of a maximum of 100, there are no negative markings for choosing the wrong answer.Once the 10 questions are answered you will get your final score, you can save this score and compete against your own score in the same category or a different one.The seven categories in this web quiz are maths,physics,chemistry,c++,java,python,aptitude.
